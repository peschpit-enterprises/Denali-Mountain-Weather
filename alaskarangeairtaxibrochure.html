Why pay for forecasts? There is seldom much value in a free mountain weather forecast. Automated computer models can provide data for all locations but they can’t consider the big picture: the weather dynamics of the surrounding areas, i.e. major troughs, cyclones forming in the Gulf of Alaska or the jet stream. Beyond geolocational data from automated systems, I examine tools such as forecast ensembles, skew-T diagrams, verification data, the ECMWF (European), the US GFS, the Global Canadian, the Global German, the UKMET (British) and local models.
